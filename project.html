AEREI [2023]

short for 'Alternative Ending Restorator: Edition I,' AEREI is a virtual humanoid of a young girl under stasis between the borders of life and death, living its life in a computer generated world based on who it was before her near death experience in 2010. it is the first successful experiment made by pseudo-medicinal tech company, CODA, who aims to extend human life beyond death through means of consciousness transfer. after months of experimentation, AEREI's operating system suddenly becomes unresponsive, cutting off funding for the AEREI project. while AEREI is left in limbo within its virtual world of Hyperspace for a decade, secrets about the ethical complications of CODA slowly come to light.

      the story of AEREI and CODA is represented through a series of websites, emails, and found footage. this ARG-like experience is currently in development.

aerei's frankenstein pack (1.19+) [2017]

an early screenshot of the pack showcasing the night sky.

introducing aerei's frankenstein pack, a 16x16 resource pack for minecraft java edition that aims to enhance minecraft's vanilla look by adding a touch of rusticism and wizardry, wrapped in a warm and comforting colour palette that is easy on the eyes. this pack offers:

  • changes to GUI and effects for a better combat and exploring experience (transparent inventory, low fire, shorter swords, no pumpkin blur, etc.)
  • seamless glass and dynamic light without the need for installing optifine
  • custom skies that include animated shooting stars and CIT items if you do have optifine
  • alternative music discs for those who enjoy more classical tracks
  • lowercase text throughout the game
  • more aesthetically pleasing animals, mobs, and paintings
  • cultural food items
  • rustic font
  • and more to come (open to suggestions!)

      as the name suggests, the frankenstein pack is a combination of many inspirations from other packs, which includes mizuno's 16 craft, excalibur, and txyme's cottagecore pack. inspiration is also drawn from animal crossing and stardew valley.

      this pack will be free for download with the release date to be determined!
