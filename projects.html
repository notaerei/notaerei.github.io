ari's web [ 2022 - now ]

the most recent layout of ari's web in 2025, hosted on github.

the first layout of "ari's web" in 2022, hosted on neocities.

you are here! ari's web is a personal site used to host everything about me, including my art, projects, interests, and more!

      originally started as a neocities blog in 2022, ari's web has transformed into a hub of creativity that allows me to express myself through a medium i grew up using as a child, while simulataneously helping me learn more about and experiment with code. i am deeply in love with the world of decentralized personal websites, and neocities was the perfect opportunity to get me back into customizing my own corner of the internet.

      eventually, ari's web moved to github in 2023 in an effort to break free from neocities' file size limits. github has allowed ari's web to grow into the home it is today with the help of github's restriction-free site hosting.

      a major goal of ari's web is to inspire others to stray away from centralized, bloated social media, and revive the art of personal websites! this project is still in development as many major features haven't been added yet to this day.

      if you are interested in keeping up with the development of this site, feel free to visit and follow my development log, or join my discord server for updates under #┃・content・creation > website development stuff.

for your consumption [ 2018 - now ]

for your consumption is an upcoming 13 track experimental album which includes various songs written, composed, produced, and performed by me. tracks from this collection date back to as early as 2018, which is when i first started making music.

      with production initially starting in 2018, the album release was delayed twice due to technical issues related to data loss throughout 2021 to 2022. after a year long hiatus from music, the album was informally announced in 2023, as production resumed. the first single from this album, "gates of heaven," was released in 2024 as a teaser.

      the multi-genre album will release sometime in late 2025 to early 2026.

ari's wardrobe [ 2025 - now ]

the first build of ari's manor in roblox.

ari's wardrobe is a roblox group founded in 2025 which aims to make customizable alternative fashion accessible for roblox players after the recent resurgence in marked up prices for user-created items. inspired by my personal fashion style, all clothing is made through a mixture of photobashing and hand-drawing to achieve a semi-realistic look.

      a homestore to showcase clothing made by me is currently in the works! this experience will also act as a social hangout game for those who enjoy the "aerei" aesthetic.

SOLIVAGANT [ 2023 - paused ]

SOLIVAGANT is an upcoming text-heavy, psychological horror game based on the face of my online identity: AEREI.

      short for 'Alternative Ending Restorator: Edition I,' AEREI is a virtual humanoid of a young girl under stasis between the borders of life and death, living its life in a computer generated world based on who it was before her near death experience in 2010. it is the first successful experiment made by pseudo-medicinal tech company, CODA, who aims to extend human life beyond death through means of consciousness transfer.

      after months of experimentation, AEREI's operating system suddenly becomes unresponsive, cutting off funding for the AEREI project. while AEREI is left in limbo within its virtual world of "Hyperspace" for a decade, secrets about the ethical complications of CODA slowly come to light.

      heavily inspired by ARGs, escape rooms, and the backrooms, the story of AEREI and CODA is represented through a series of websites, emails, and found footage, alongside a playable game.

      the development of this experience is currently paused while i prioritize other projects.

aespador [ 2015 - paused ]

aespador is an indie rpg game about navigating life in a post-war continent. centered around playing as an "Arcan" with magical capabilities, your journey will be full of slice of life elements, combat, dialogue rich interactions with characters, branching decisions with different endings, and a generally story heavy experience. aespador is heavily inspired by stardew valley, hades, hollow knight, and don't starve.

      development has been paused to prioritize other projects. when development resumes, volunteer and paid positions will be opened for this project.

ari's frankenstein pack (1.19+) [ 2017 - paused ]

aerei's frankenstein pack is a 16x16 resource pack for minecraft java edition that aims to enhance minecraft's vanilla look by adding a touch of rusticism and wizardry, wrapped in a warm and comforting colour palette that is easy on the eyes. this pack offers:

  • changes to GUI and effects for a better combat and exploring experience (transparent inventory, low fire, shorter swords, no pumpkin blur, etc.)
  • seamless glass and dynamic light without the need for installing optifine
  • custom skies that include animated shooting stars and CIT items if you do have optifine
  • alternative music discs for those who enjoy more classical tracks
  • lowercase text throughout the game with a rustic font
  • more aesthetically pleasing animals, mobs, and paintings
  • cultural food items
  • and more to come!

      as the name suggests, ari's frankenstein pack is a combination of many inspirations from other packs, which includes mizuno's 16 craft, excalibur, and txyme's cottagecore pack. inspiration is also drawn from animal crossing and stardew valley.

      this pack will be free for download! however, development has been paused due to an inability to keep up with minecraft's frequency of updates, as well as prioritization of other projects.
