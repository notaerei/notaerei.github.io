2. commission ordering process . . .

first, you must have references already prepared through links before you fill out an order form. i will accept reference sheets and written descriptions, but i will not come up with huge ideas from scratch for you without an additional negotiable charge. i will not drastically change my art style to look like someone else's, nor will i knowingly copy ideas, so all my work will have my own personal spin put upon it. my art style must be kept in mind before commissioning me.

to initiate the ordering process, you will fill out the order form found on my website to place a commission request. this form documents the official details of the request and is stored indefinitely in my personal drive.

upon receiving your request, i will reach out to you approving or denying your order. after confirming the details and price of your commission through private communication, i will set up an invoice for you to pay in full before i start working on your request. the information provided through the order form will be posted on my public to-do list. any extra relevant details about the request provided through private communication will also be added to my to-do list.

my work process is broken down into two steps: the "just started/rough sketch" phase, where you are able to suggest changes and provide as much feedback as you want, then the "halfway done" phase and onwards, where i will not allow any further major edits unless it's related to colour.

when approximately half of the work has been done, i will provide you with an estimate of the date of completion. if i am unable to deliver the product on that provided date, i will provide you with a new estimation of a completion date. i will deliver the final product when the work is fully completed. if you indicate in your order form that you must receive the product by a specific date, and i do not deliver on time, i will offer you a significant discount on any future orders you make with me.

if you abandon communication with me while i am working on your commission, i will attempt to contact you once a week. if i do not receive any response after these two attempts, i will consider the project abandoned or cancelled, and will no longer resume work.
