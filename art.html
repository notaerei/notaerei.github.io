loyalty points . . .

loyalty points (or "stars") are an additional way for me to say "thank you" to those who consistently support me by commissioning me multiple times!

how it works . . .

after commissioning me, you automatically receive...

  • 20 ★ for every bug eyed character
  • 30 ★ for every semi realistic character
  • 5 ★ for every emote
  • 1 ★ for every badge
  • 20 ★ for every thumbnail
  • 10-20 ★ for stream gfx (varies)

when filling out an order form for your next commission, you can mention that you want to use your star(s) to get a discount on the final price. if you're unsure how many stars you have, check this list!

rules of the stars . . .

you will not get stars from the commission you are using your stars on. in order to build up stars, you will have to buy a commission without using your stars. you also do not have to use all of your stars immediately. you can save them for as long as you want!
