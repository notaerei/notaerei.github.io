2024 was a year of busying myself so much to the point of exhaustion, creative block, and overall fatigue from art. i often felt that i was only ever able to work on art if it was related to a commission order, or a youtube video. actively participating in artfight this year was a huge accomplishment for me because it was the first time in years i felt i was able to put aside time from my hectic life to focus on making art purely for fun. although i didn't do many art studies this year, i certainly experimented and played with colours, effects, and slight adjustments to my art styles. please enjoy perusing through my few, but fun, pieces from 2024.
