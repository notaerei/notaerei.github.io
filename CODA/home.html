Regarding AEREI.

With a heavy heart, we are saddened to confirm that as of April 21, 2012, our most publicized build of exodOS (better known as 'AEREI') is permanently, and irreversibly inoperable due to a data breach within our Southern California headquarters.

Initially conceived on December 30, 2010, AEREI was our most publicized build, and a very beloved project at CODA. Being CODA's debut project, the development of AEREI has been a significantly crucial stepping stone in kickstarting our research. For the past two years, we have been able to break barriers in the fields of end-of-life care, neuroscience, and artificial intelligence in ways that wouldn't have been possible without AEREI.

We hope this update brings you the closure you may be searching for. From all of us here at CODA, we thank you endlessly for your support.
