a note from the curator . . .

right in the heart of the pandemic, 2021 was a year full of stylistic experimentation, fixating over genshin impact, and regularly making content on youtube. this marks one of the last years that i had ample time to create art, as well as one of the last years that traditional art was my primary method of drawing. as i transitioned from traditional and mixed media to fully digital art by slowly switching over to krita as my main drawing program, i managed to experiment with different ways of portraying anatomy using high contrast colours from a singular colour palette that i curated over time. 2021 was also the year that i transitioned from a soft lineart style that i developed from paint tool sai to a harsher and sharper lineart style that i adopted by using krita. overall, 2021 was a great and fun transitional period in both my real life and my art life.
