2023 was the year i started to switch more to using krita for my art! while it definitely was a learning curve after using paint tool sai for so long, i feel like the quality of my art improved drastically since krita allowed more creative freedom that paint tool sai hindered from how limited its features are. also, 2023 was a year that i started to indulge in drawing AEREI more as a character rather than a manifestation of myself as a person. so, i ended up drawing AEREI a lot more for fun as a way to practice getting used to her design, as it is very different from my 'aereisona' from previous years. lastly, there is a little more art that i did this year that is available to view on patreon. enjoy!
