how it works . . .

after commissioning me, you automatically receive...

  • 20 ★ for every bug eyed character
  • 30 ★ for every semi realistic character
  • 5 ★ for every emote

you do not get stars from the commission you are using your stars on. in order to build up stars, you will have to buy a commission without using your stars. you also do not have to use all of your stars immediately. you can save them for as long as you want!
