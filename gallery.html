please note . . .


please keep in mind that i have certain boundaries with what i will and will not draw.

i will not draw . . .

  • explicit nsfw / sex / porn (not in my skillset, and not the kind of art i want to create)

  • discriminatory statements, especially those that disagree with my personal views
  • real life political figures or celebrities (not comfortable with it however, fictional characters based off of real life people are fine)

i will definitely draw . . .

  • original or fan characters, including characters from shows, games, or any similar types of media

  • light gore
  • furniture

terms of service . . .

IMPORTANT... please read through this entire page before buying a commission. by commissioning me, you are confirming that you have already read and agreed to the following:

how to order a commission from me . . .

  1. fill out the order form found at the bottom of this page! please do not DM me your commission request because it can get lost in my inbox. though, if you have any questions or concerns about the form, feel free to contact me!
  2. i will contact you approving or denying your request. after confirming the details and price of your commission, i will wait for payment. (please refer to the payment rules below.)
  3. there are usually two stages to my art process : the "just started/rough sketch" phase, where you are allowed to make changes and provide as much feedback as you want, and the "halfway done" phase or onwards, where i will not allow any further major edits at this stage unless it's related to colour.
  4. when i'm finished, i will send you the final product! please refer to the terms of use below if you plan to use my art online.

what you will receive . . .

  • a private google drive folder to download the finished product at the highest quality possible.
  • all files are rendered in .png. please specify if you would like a different file type in your order form, but note that i will not send .pdf, .kra, or .sai files.
  • canvas sizes by default depend on the request. please specify if you would like an alternate canvas size in your order form.

payment info . . .

  • all payments MUST be made through venmo or paid by a paypal invoice that i will send to you. the invoice will have a deadline of 7 days, but if you need this date extended, please let me know.
  • all prices listed are in USD and must be converted to your local currency if you live outside of the U.S. i do not charge local tax.
  • i will start working on your commission once i receive payment. if i do not receive payment within 1 week of confirming your request and you do not communicate with me that you want your payment deadline extended, i will assume you have cancelled your request and i will not work on it. you will have to contact me again to resume the commission process. there will be no need to submit another form unless you are commissioning something else entirely!
  • please do not send commission payments without my approval! i might consider it a tip or donation.
  • i can negotiate prices depending on the situation but please be respectful if i happen to deny additional discounts.
  • payments are final, no refunds.

things to know before ordering . . .

  • please check my to-do list to get a rough estimate of how busy i am before buying art from me! if my commissions are closed, i may have a delayed response to your request. please be patient with me if this is the case!
  • i will not change my art style to look like someone else's nor will i blatantly copy ideas. if you want me to take heavy inspiration from something, please know i will likely put my own spin on it to make it more original to avoid copyright infringement. keep my art style in mind before commissioning me.
  • please have references already prepared before you fill out an order form! i accept reference sheets, written descriptions, pinterest boards, etc. i will not come up with ideas from scratch for you unless it is a small detail where you allow me creative freedom.
  • i have the right to reject any commission or put you on hold temporarily. please do not take this personally!

terms of use . . .

  • i own the rights to my own art. just because you pay for it doesn't necessarily mean you can claim it as yours.
  • my art is for personal use only and is not meant for commercial use. if you want to use my art for commercial use, please talk about it with me first. additionally, my art may not be used in AI generators, nor altered in any way using AI.
  • do not edit or trace my art unless you are doing so privately to practice developing your own art. even then, i would appreciate if you asked me first. if you are making minor edits (adding text, recolouring, animating, etc.) please contact me for permission before doing so.
  • i have the right to post my art publicly online right after i give you the google drive link, but please let me know if you do not want me to do so beforehand.
  • you MUST give me credit if you are using or posting my art anywhere. no exceptions. you can do this by including my social media link(s) in your bio, caption, description, etc. my watermark is only partially visible on most of my art in order to maintain the integrity of the piece, so providing further credit helps me a lot! if you are still unsure how or where to credit me (especially if you want to make it more discreet or subtle), feel free to talk to me about it!
