latest youtube video . . .

      a few weeks ago, one of my favourite musicals ever, jesus christ superstar, was performed by the wonderful cynthia erivo and adam lambert (among many other equally wonderful performers) which inspired me to make this cover! this song is wildly out of my range and was really only covered just for fun, i wasn't really intending on uploading it.

latest twitch stream . . .

      ever since i've moved to a new place, i have been able to stream a little more consistently than i used to! although my streaming schedule isn't anywhere near finalized, i'm very grateful i get to sit down at least once a week to go live. in this stream, i initially started working on a random concept i had in mind, which then devolved into reacting to persona music i had never heard of, then challenging myself to recreate a song from memory without hearing what i'm making. it's very likely that this VOD will be turned into a youtube video, it was a very fun night!
